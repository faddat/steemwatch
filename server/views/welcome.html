Welcome to SteemWatch

Hear my words and bear witness to my vow. Steem grows, and now my watch begins.

SteemWatch is the notification center for Steem you have always dreamed about. It lets you pick Steem events you are interested in and then dispatch notifications using some of the notification mechanisms available. No longer you need to keep reloading Steemit.com. Just get notified automatically when the content you are interested in appears, you story gets upvoted and more!

You can sign into SteemWatch using one of the following providers. SteemWatch is only going to read and remember your email address, nothing else. Please note that every account is bound to an email address, so in case you decide to use multiple login providers and your email address differs across these services, you will get a new serparate account for every distinct email address encountered.
